Drawing

Pencil by Iamthestig from commons.wikimedia.org. CC0

I have been drawing for a long time. I do it when I have nothing to do, or when an idea just pops into my head! I take drawing classes and I also like sculpting and things involving paper. Many of my drawings include people, birds, and some nature. Drawing can calm yourself down, and it is very fun to do.

Science

Atom art by ScienceDiscovererfrom commons.wikipedia.org. CC-BY-SA

I am interested in science. I love conducting experiments, learning something I did not know, and discovering new things. I ask lots of questions, and I learn very quickly. That's what my friends say, at least.

Writing

I love writing and making up short stories. I like writing for fun but I don't like writing essays for school or something specific that I have to do. That's VERY boring. My favorite genres are fiction and biography, because I like reading and learning about people I didn't know before.

My favorite animals

I love animals. I used to have fish, and I wish that my family had a dog (I might even get one soon). Here are some of my favorites.

Tigers

I like tigers because I think that their stripes look very cool, and tiger cubs are very cute. Tiger moms are very protective of their babies.
